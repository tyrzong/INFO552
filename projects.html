Projects

Daily Haiku

"Daily Haiku" is an iOS app that I have created using SwiftUI and Core Data, designed to ignite daily moments of poetic inspiration. As the creator of this app, I use it every day to effortlessly craft traditional 5-7-5 haikus, guided by its seamless syllable counting feature. Witness your poetic journey unfold, capturing nature's beauty, emotions, and life's essence in just seventeen syllables.

Cloud Carbon Footprint

"Cloud Carbon Footprint" was a project that I was working on at my last job. I created data pipelines connecting to different public cloud providers and gathered all the carbon emission data. It then analyzes carbon emissions and creates a report. I aim to promote environmental accountability and foster a sustainable digital landscape. Together, let's drive positive change for a greener planet.

Caretaker IOS

CareGiver iOS is a task-based notification system that is triggered by Bluetooth beacons placed in strategic locations. The CareGivee, a user with an intellectual disability, receives a notification as well as instructions to complete the task when they come into contact with the Bluetooth beacon. This was my Senior desing project which placed top 4 for my year.
