Caretaker IOS

CareGiver iOS is a task-based notification system that is triggered by Bluetooth beacons placed in strategic locations. The CareGivee, a user with an intellectual disability, receives a notification as well as instructions to complete the task when they come into contact with the Bluetooth beacon.
